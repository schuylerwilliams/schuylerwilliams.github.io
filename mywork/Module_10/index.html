Where Are We Going?

The map - depicting data from Individual income tax returns filed with the IRS - approximates the number of residents leaving a state and which state they moved to. Each value is a percentage of the total people moving from the selected state. Lighter shades indicate low percentages, while darker shades indicate higher percentages. Red indicates a zero value.

Hover over the chart or the map to retrieve percentages. Click a state, bar, or choose from the dropdown in the top left corner to update the data to outflows from the selected state.
