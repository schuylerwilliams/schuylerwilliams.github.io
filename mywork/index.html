Colorado Hospitals Map

This map displays Colorado Hospital information by county. The GIS layers were organized and analyzed with ArcMap, while the majority of the styling was done in Adobe Illustrator. Each circle represents a county. The larger the left side of the circle, the greater the number of hospitals in that county. The larger the right side of the circle, the greater the hospital bed count per county. The color of the circle indicates the highest Trauma center level available in the county.

European Political Map

This map is a simple European Political Map, and was one of the first maps that I completed. It displays each country in Europe, as well as labeling capitals, major cities, major riverways, and major water features.

Women Political Map

This map displays the percentage of seats in National Parliament by country occupied by women. The color indicates the percentage, to the max of 49%, to the low of 3%. The inset map help aids in the analysis of the smaller countries in Latin America.
